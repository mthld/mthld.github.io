I'm a GNU/Linux & UNIX sysadmin currently working in a french co-op, Clic2000, with my friend Myrdin.

I enjoy walking outside, sometimes roller-skating, riding a (mountain-)bike or Muay Thai boxing. But you'll usually find me cooking food, hugging my partner or being angry at some stuff I have no control at. While of course listening to some good acid techno tracks, hardcore or rap/trap songs.
